## Hi, I'm Eliza 👋

I'm a sociology MA student at Minzu University of China,
with a BA in Sociology (GPA 4.06/5, 2020–2024).

My research sits at the intersection of **ethnic minority communities**,
**rural development**, and **digital ethnography** — with a particular
interest in how culture, governance, and everyday life intersect in
China's borderland regions.

## Research Interests

- Rural-cultural-tourism integration in ethnic minority areas
- Digital fieldwork & online ethnography
- Traditional aesthetics, symbolic appropriation & cultural capital
- Grassroots governance & community resilience

## Selected Projects

- *"Knowing the Field"* — Fieldwork on agri-cultural-tourism development
  in a minority village, Yunnan (2025)
- *Digital Ethnography Training Camp*, Shanghai Normal University (2025)
  — Paper: *Symbolic Appropriation of Traditional Chinese Aesthetics
  and Cultural Capital Transformation*
- Fieldwork in Pu'er, Yunnan — Ethnic solidarity & border community
  research (2023)
