## Eliza 陈仪真
**MA in Sociology @ Minzu University of China** | Organizational & Digital Sociologist

I'm a sociologist exploring how digital technologies reshape social organization and relationships. Currently pursuing my Master's degree, I conduct fieldwork examining emerging organizational forms—particularly how decentralized systems transform trust-building, skill-sharing, and community formation in rural contexts.

My research interests lie at the intersection of **organizational sociology** and **technology sociology**, with a specific focus on understanding how technologies, organizations, and social relationships co-evolve and mutually shape each other.
